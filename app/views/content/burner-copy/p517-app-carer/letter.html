If their benefits include a severe disability premium

If your Carer’s Allowance claim is paid, {{ dpName }} will stop getting any severe disability premium

A severe disability premium is an extra amount of money that may be added to their:

  • Income Support
  • income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance (JSA)
  • income-related Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Housing Benefit

If they get Pension Credit

If {{ dpName }} is paid an extra amount of Pension Credit for severe disability, this will stop if your Carer’s Allowance claim is paid.

If they pay a reduced rate of Council Tax

Their Council Tax reduction could be affected if your Carer’s Allowance claim is successful. You can check this with their local council at:
www.gov.uk/find-local-council

If you need to speak to someone about their benefits

Contact the department that deals with their benefit if you need to check whether Carer’s Allowance will affect their payments.
