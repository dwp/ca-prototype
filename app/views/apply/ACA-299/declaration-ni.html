Declaration

By sending this application you agree that:

  • the information you have given is complete and correct, as far as you know
  • the Disablity and Carers Service will check your record and the record of the person you care for on internal systems
  • the Disability and Carers Service may check your information with other government departments
  • you will pay back any money you have been overpaid if you are asked to
  • you will report changes that affect your claim as soon as possible using the report a change to Carer’s Allowance online service (opens in a new window or tab), or contact the Disability and Carers Service to report changes by phone or post (opens in a new window or tab)
