Where should we pay your Carer's Allowance?

You can have your Carer's Allowance paid into a UK bank or building society account, either belonging to you or someone else.

If you choose to have it paid into someone else's account:

  • you are still responsible for repaying any overpayments
  • you must have their permission to be paid into their account
  • they must use your money how you tell them to
