Carer's Allowance claim start date

You can backdate your claim for up to 3 months, as long as:

  • the person you are caring for was getting their disability benefit
  • you were providing care for at least 35 hours a week
Rules about the Carer's Allowance claim date
You can backdate your claim for more than 3 months if the person you care for was awarded their disability benefit in the last 3 months and the backdated start date of that benefit was more than 3 months ago.

In this scenario, your claim date can be backdated to either:

  • their benefit start date - if you were providing care for 35 hours a week from then
  • the date you started to provide care - if this was after their benefit start date

Your claim date cannot be more than 3 months in the future.
