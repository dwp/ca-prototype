Your claim has been sent

Your reference number is your National Insurance number

What happens next

You need to send the documents below straight away. DWP can’t consider awarding your benefit without them.

You’ll usually get a decision in 6 weeks, but this may take longer. There will be a further delay if you don’t send the required documents.

You will be notified by letter once a decision has been made. If further documents are needed you will be contacted by phone or letter.

You do not need to contact DWP.

Documents required

You must now send:

  • last payslip you got before 26 June 2019 (your claim date)
  • any payslips you have had since your claim date
  • your P45

You can send original documents or photocopies.

Send your documents to:

Carer’s Allowance Unit
Mail Handling Site A
Wolverhampton
WV98 2AB

Include your name, address and National Insurance number with anything you send.
