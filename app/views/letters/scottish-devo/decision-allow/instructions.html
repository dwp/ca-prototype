Instructions

1. Select some text you want to highlight

You can click and drag with your mouse over the text if you are using one. If you are using a phone or tablet you will need to hold your finger down on the text and use the on-screen controls.

2. Press either the “Makes sense” or “Does not make sense” button

These buttons will highlight the phrase you have selected so we can understand if you understand or have trouble understanding that phrase.

Examples

  • This sentence is highlighted as ‘Makes sense’.
  • This sentence is highlighted as ‘Does not make sense’.

3. Repeat until you have finished giving your feedback

You might need to scroll the page down to see more of the paragraphs and sections.

4. Press “I am finished”

The researcher will explain what to do next.
