A break in care is any time you have not been caring for John Smith for at least 35 hours in a week. For Carer's Allowance, a week is classed as Sunday to Saturday

This can include times where you or John Smith were:

  • in hospital
  • in respite or a care home
  • on holiday

You do not need to tell us about any of the above if you continued to care for John Smith for 35 hours or more a week during that time.
