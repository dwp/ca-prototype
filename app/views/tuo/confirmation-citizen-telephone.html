What happens next

You must complete Tell Us Once by 28 March 2022.

If Tell Us Once is not completed by 28 March 2022, we will delete the death registration details you have given us.

How to complete Tell Us Once

You can complete Tell Us Once by telephone, free of charge.

  • Telephone: 0800 085 7308
  • Textphone: 0800 141 2218
  • Monday to Friday, 8am to 5pm

You can also complete Tell Us Once online at www.gov.uk/tell-us-once

What you will need

You will need:

  • your Tell Us Once reference number
  • the name and date of birth of the person who has died
  • the name and address of the hospital care home or hospice if they died there
  • details of the surviving spouse
  • National Insurance nubmber or date of birth of the surviving spouse
  • details of the next of kin (closest relative by blood or marriage)
  • details of the person or company dealing with the estate (property, belongings and money)
! Warning You must have consent from the next of kin or the person or company dealing with the estate to give their details.

Depending on who you want us to tell, you may need the following details of the person who has died:

  • passport number
  • town of birth
  • National Insurance number
  • driving licence number
  • vehicle registrations numbers of any vehicles they owned
  • name of their local council and services they were getting, such as Housing Benefit or Council Tax reduction
  • vehicle registrations numbers of any vehicles they owned
