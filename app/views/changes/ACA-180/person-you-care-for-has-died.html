If the death has not been registered yet

You can contact the Bereavement Service to let DWP know the person you care for has died. They can also give advice on bereavement benefits.

If the death has been registered

If you registered the death, the registrar would have let you know if you could use the Tell Us Once service to report the death. This updates most government departments, including DWP.

If you cannot use Tell Us Once, you can still contact the Bereavement Service to let DWP know the person you care for has died.
